Manga Club Bot

Mika-Chan

When I founded the Manga Club at UB in 2020, I designed and deployed this currently online and working bot to welcome new users of Manga Club on the Discord server.

Fun fact: it is due to Mika-chan that the Manga Club at University at Buffalo continues to exist despite the pandemic. Mika-chan was designed to not only greet users but also gather their emails and save them as members of the club. These saved emails were reached out to in the future who ended up being the future leadership to keep the club alive!
